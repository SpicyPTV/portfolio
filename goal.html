My goal

A look at what I want the future of the Web to be.

A Sustainable Web

A self supporting and governing web is the future. Free from the laws and restrictions of it's countries real world laws and regulations. Information and knowledge should be free and readily avalible for all who want to access them not hidden away behind paywalls or password protection

A Secure Web

Security is paramount for the future of the web. If the avergae user is not fully protected from people with malicious intent why should they use the web? They should feel completely safe going from site to site withought worrying that they could be attacked. Expanding on built is browser and search engine security coul make malicious links a think of the past.

A free Web

A web with way more control other the trackers and cookies being used on you at all times is needed and should really already be in place
